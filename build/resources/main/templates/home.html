Home
My Account
This section enables to view/edit your details. You can see the projects that you own and the projects that you participate in. You can also view the tasks that have been assigned to you.
Projects
This section enables you to view all the projects that you own or you participate in. It will also enable you to click on a project to view the tasks associated with it and its other details.
Reports
In this section, you can generate reports to view the progress of tasks and projects. You can select a particular project to generate its report. This will help to track its status.
Invitations
This section enables you to view the pending invitations sent by other users to participate in other projects. You can choose to either accept or reject an invitation.
